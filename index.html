About Jubilee Health Insurance

Our Heritage

Jubilee Health Insurance Company of Uganda Ltd (JHICU) is part of the Jubilee Insurance Companies across East Africa, first incorporated on 3rd August 1937 in Mombasa, Kenya.

Local Presence

In 1940, Jubilee Insurance opened a full branch office in Kampala. On 23rd July 1991, Jubilee was locally incorporated in Uganda, serving our community for over 30 years.

Our Commitment

We provide first-class health insurance products including inpatient, out-patient, maternity, dental, optical coverage and comprehensive scheme administration.
